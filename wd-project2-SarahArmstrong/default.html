Meet the Owner

My name is Sarah Armstrong and I've been decorating cakes for the last 5 years. I'm from San Antonio, Tx but currently reside in Schertz, TX were my shop is located also. I enjoy making cakes for a varity of occasions but my specialty is cakes for kids birthdays.

If you are looking for just a cake, I am able to make the cake by itself. If you happen to be hosting for a large group of people I can make matching cup cakes to acommodate your cake. Whatever you need, I'm your girl! Please take a look around my site and I hope to hear from you soon.
